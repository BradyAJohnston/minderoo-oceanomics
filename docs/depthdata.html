Estimating Line Shape

While the descent had some time points where there was a pause, the ascent stage was consistent until the first nisken was recovered at ~ 50 m depth.

The winch operated at ~ 100 m / 2 minutes, or a speed of 50 m / min.

Just the ascent stage shows two distinct ascent pre and post 13:40, or above and below ~250 m.

df %>% 
  filter(
    direction == "up"
  ) %>% 
  mutate(depth = -depth) %>% 
  e_charts(time) %>% 
  e_line(depth)

During ascent, the winch was stopped for the top nisken bottle at a ~ 25 metres as 5 of them were spaced 5 metres apart.

The winch then pulled ~975 m of line in ~ 22 minutes, at a rate of ~ 44 m / minute.
